Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions and Guide

How do I record a new purchase?

Go to the "New Purchase" section from your sidebar. Fill in the customer name, phone number, product name, ID, and bill number. Click "Save" to register the purchase.

How do I process a return?

Navigate to "Return Request". Enter the bill number provided by the customer. If the purchase is found, fill in the return date and reason, then submit the request.

What are the refund statuses?

Pending: Request is recorded and waiting for review.
Approved: Manager has approved the refund, payment processing initiated.
Rejected: Refund request was declined.
Completed: Refund has been fully processed and money returned.

Need further assistance? Contact our technical support at support@return-ease.com
