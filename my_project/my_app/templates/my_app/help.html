Help & Support

Need assistance? We're here to help you navigate through Return-Ease.

How do I start a refund?

Go to your products page, select the item, and click "Initiate Refund". Follow the on-screen instructions.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect all your transaction and personal data.

When will I get my money back?

Processing times vary by merchant, but we track every step and notify you immediately upon any update.
